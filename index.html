How It Works

Three Steps to
In-Scene Placement

Our AI analyzes every frame, identifies placeable surfaces and objects, then renders brand assets with photorealistic lighting and perspective.

01

Scan & Detect

AI processes each frame to identify surfaces, objects, lighting conditions, depth maps, and occlusion boundaries in real time.

02

Match & Bid

Brands bid on detected placement opportunities via programmatic auction. AI matches brand assets to scene context, viewer demographics, and content tone.

03

Render & Deliver

Products are composited into the scene with matched lighting, perspective correction, and natural occlusion. Personalized per viewer, served at the edge.
